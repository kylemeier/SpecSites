Reservations

PLEASE NOTE: Cancellations for any reason will result in a two year ban from our establishment

Reservations are made by knocking rhythmically on our front door. For private kale service, please provide a creative knock. Our standard service will be provided to those who provide standard knocks.

Once you have knocked, be prepared to answer a sphinxian riddle. You will not be allowed to consult outside sources for assistance.

If you answer the riddle correctly, write your name on the wait list in pencil (artisan pencils only please). You are then free to go home and wait for our call within the year.
