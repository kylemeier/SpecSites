Our Story

Kale has been in our family dating back to the middle ages. Nowadays, we specialize in fresh Cavolo Nero, or Brassica oleracea acephala, that we ship daily straight from the sun kissed valleys of Tuscany. In order to ensure safe passage, we personally accompany the Cavolo Nero on its trip from Tuscany into our doors.

However, we also must ensure the Cavolo Nero on the flight is of the utmost quality. To achieve this, we personally inspect each leaf of Cavolo Nero before allowing it on the flight. Any leaf that does not pass inspection is placed carefully into a biodegradable bag and thrown into the jet engine at altitude to prevent any accidental human consumption.

Once on American soil, we provide the Cavolo Nero with the industry standard spa, massage, and sunbathe regime. Finally, we deep fry it to mask all flavor because, lets be honest, kale tastes awful.
